WHAT IS MAHJRANK?

Your Mahjong game, finally measured.

MahjRank brings Elo-based skill ratings to in-person American Mahjong for the first time. Whether you play at a club, a kitchen table, or a tournament — every game counts.

📊

Elo-Rated Rankings

The same system used in chess, adapted for 4-player Mahjong. Beat stronger players, gain more points.

🏅

27 Badges & Achievements

Win streaks, milestones, jokerless victories — all tracked automatically. Share your achievements.

🏠

Club Management

Create your club, manage members, track your group's stats. Club leaderboards let groups compete.

📱

Works Like an App

Install right from your browser. Log games at the table in seconds. Push notifications keep you updated.
